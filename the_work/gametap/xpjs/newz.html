Action Blast Land Re-release

Tank Granite, working in conjunction with his good friends at Canadian Chan Publishing plans to release a special anniversary edition of Action Blast Land, Tank's critically acclaimed foray into the world of Platform Jumping. There is no release date as of this writing, but Tank's spokesperson says it should be hitting stores really soon.

Jumping Dubbed Official Sport of Gameland City

Thanks to some serious lobbying on the behalf of Tank Granite and thousands of jumping fans everywhere, legislators have named jumping the official sport of Gameland City. Several politicians called this move "long overdue," while Tank called it "sweet justice".

Tank Speaks To Graduates

Tank Granite recently spoke to the graduating class of 2005 at Game City University. No stranger to commencements, Tank told the young people to "stay smart, know the rules, get serious, and jump really high." The remarks were met with an impressive 40 second standing ovation.

Xtreme Platform Jumping School Makes Top 10. Again.

XPJS was recently named one of the top technical schools in the country by The Newsworthy Standard. Coming in second for the third year in a row, XPJS was described as the best school in the country to learn jumping and ducking.
