We'd like to say thanks for making 7/10/10 one of the happiest days of our lives! Despite some weather-releated mishaps (torrential rain, widespread flooding in Somerville and Fenway Park, a woman 5 minutes away from the wedding went kayaking in the street, etc etc), we managed to celebrate and catch up with so many of you. It was a great time, and we'd like to thank you by giving a little bit back. Here are a few digital party favors.

Background Music

Did you hear something you liked at the wedding or rehearsal dinner? Kenji spent a lot of time making playlists, and wanted to share his efforts with you.

  1. Rehearsal Dinner mix, 69 tracks. A rowdier collection than the music played on 7/10/10, and a longer mix as well - just a shade over 4 hours. A good selection of the classics from the mid-60s to the mid-70s - Beatles, Stones, Neil Young, Fleetwood Mac, Bowie, Lou Reed.

    Download: mp3s in a zipped folder, 378 MB.

  2. Wedding Day mix, 47 tracks. All the non-live music from our wedding day: guest arrival tunes (1950s jazz), the bridesmaids' walk (Introduction by Nick Drake from 1970's Bryter Layter), Lauren's trip down the aisle (This Will Be Our Year by The Zombies from 1968's Odessey and Oracle), and the cocktail hour (early 1960s rock and soul).

    Download: mp3s in a zipped folder, 253 MB.
